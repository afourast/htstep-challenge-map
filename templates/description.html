HT-Step Benchmark: Temporal Article Grounding.

Task

Given an instructional article, consisting of a sequence of step descriptions, and a video demonstrating an activity, temporal article grounding refers to detecting the temporal segments in the video where these steps appear.

Dataset

The HT-Step benchmark is a collection of temporal annotations on videos from the HowTo100M dataset. The annotations are a sequence of steps taken from a WikiHow article. Each step consists of a headline sentence and a paragraph describing the step in more detail. Some steps of the article may not appear at all, appear partially, be executed in different order, or be repeated multiple times.
